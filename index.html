DATADONORS

A platform that collects and distributes health data over entire lifetimes, providing individuals and the global scientiﬁc community with unprecedented access to detailed records of health and lifestyle activity.

More

What Is Datadonors?

We're used to sharing everything online. It's what we do everyday. Using Facebook, Twitter, Runkeeper, Fitbit or whatever platform, we share all the time. But does that really help anyone?

In most cases, it doesn't. And that's ok. But what if we could save lives with that same sharing? By connecting any of these devices and apps and sharing your information, you can help us to create an extensive database that can be used by researchers to improve health and eradicate illness.

A campaign by The Wikilife Foundation, a non-profit §501(c)(3) organization.

Why Should You Share?

For the Research Community: Help them discover and create new hypotheses to understand and improve our health.

For the World: Your health data is a piece of the puzzle that helps us to see the picture of global health.

For Yourself: Get all your health and lifestyle information in one simple and secure place and compare with others.
